Product Overview

Built for every
family in the
Tri-State area.

Remodel NYC+CT is a premium digital platform connecting homeowners across New York City, Westchester, and Fairfield County, CT with a trusted luxury remodeling contractor. Prospects browse a curated portfolio, review compliance credentials, and submit detailed inquiries — all in a single session.

Public Website — Portfolio browser, inquiry form, compliance credentials, bilingual toggle

Client App — Project dashboard, document signing, real-time messaging, compliance tracker

Admin Dashboard — Project management, permit tracking, client communication hub

Dark Warm Background
#1A1007 — a near-black warm brown used throughout. Dark backgrounds communicate exclusivity and premium positioning, consistent with luxury real estate and high-end interior brands. 12:1 contrast ratio for all body text.
📐
Left Vertical Copper Accent
A consistent copper/terracotta vertical bar anchors every content slide and screen on the left — creating visual continuity and a proprietary brand signature that users recognize across every touchpoint.
✍️
Serif + Sans Pairing
Cormorant Garamond for display and headings — timeless, editorial, warm. DM Sans for body and UI — clean, modern, accessible. The pairing communicates both heritage craftsmanship and digital precision.
🎯
Copper as Interaction Signal
All interactive elements — CTAs, form fields, navigation links, and accents — use copper consistently. Users learn in seconds that copper means "act here." This leverages Gestalt Similarity for zero learning curve.

Gestalt Applied

Visual psychology
drives every decision.

Classic perceptual principles govern layout, grouping, and hierarchy — reducing cognitive load and guiding all three personas naturally through each flow.

01
Proximity
Grouped Form Clusters
On the inquiry form, personal details (name, email, phone) form one cluster; project details (type, budget, timeline) a second. Users perceive each cluster as a unit — reducing cognitive load and increasing completion rates.
02
Similarity
Copper = Interactive
Every interactive element shares the copper accent color. Users recognize immediately that copper means "tap here" — no labels required. A consistent visual grammar that works for Meg, Carlos, and Jim alike.
03
Figure / Ground
Dark Field Hierarchy
Dark background creates a clear figure-ground separation. Content cards and CTA buttons sit as foreground figures. The depth layering lets users focus on what matters without visual noise.

Iterative Design

Two changes.
Every persona served.

Usability testing revealed two critical gaps. Both were addressed before the high-fidelity prototype — task completion jumped to 100%.

01
Language Toggle Added
Before — Original Design
English-only inquiry form. Assumption: luxury positioning implied English-speaking audience. Carlos noted it "felt like it wasn't built for us."
After — Revised Design
Full EN/ES toggle in the navigation bar. All form fields, labels, and CTAs switch language. Bilingual inquiry submissions increased immediately in Round 2 testing.
02
Budget Field + Accessible Labels
Before — Original Design
No budget range field on inquiry form. 9px form labels failed VoiceOver testing. Jim Harrington could not complete the form independently.
After — Revised Design
Budget tier selector added. All labels raised to 14px minimum with WCAG AA contrast. VoiceOver task completion rate: 100% in Round 2. Form completion rate: 100% across all personas.

Investor Roadmap

Three steps to
market launch.

From validated prototype to a live Tri-State platform — a disciplined, phased approach with measurable milestones.

01
Phase 1 · Q1 2026
Extended Usability Testing
Conduct high-fidelity usability testing with 10+ participants across all three persona groups. Focus on screen reader flow completion, bilingual form submission accuracy, and client dashboard task completion. Validate the 100% completion rate achieved in Round 2 at scale.
Target: 95%+ task completion across all 3 personas
02
Phase 2 · Q2 2026
Client Portal & Admin Dashboard — Full Hi-Fi
Develop the client portal and admin dashboard to full high-fidelity. Implement real project tracking, document e-signing, permit milestone notifications, and the compliance credential display. Build the admin-side contractor workflow: project intake, client assignment, and permit status automation.
Deliverable: Full interactive prototype · Client + Admin
03
Phase 3 · Q3 2026
Live Platform Launch
Partner with the Remodel NYC+CT contractor to launch the production platform. Track inquiry completion rate, bilingual form submissions, and accessibility task-completion as primary success metrics. Implement A/B testing on the portfolio filter and inquiry form to optimize conversion. Target markets: Greenwich, CT → Westchester → NYC boroughs.
KPIs: Inquiry completion · Bilingual % · Accessibility pass rate
